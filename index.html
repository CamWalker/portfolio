About Me

Full-stack Javascript Developer | ReactJS, AngularJS, Node.js and RESTful APIs

Valuable ideas have been the key to success in my career.

I am a self-taught programmer. It started as a single idea—improve a tedious process at work. I was working in operations at Goldman Sachs, and I became fed up with relying on technology for solutions. So, within the space of a year I taught myself to program in VBA and improved tens of processes, saving hours of daily manual processing.

But I couldn't stop there.

My desire to learn programming and make ideas a reality led me to seek more education in programming. I attended Dev Mountain, a coding boot camp, where I thrived. My previous experience with programming prepared me immensely for the rapid instruction in web development. Not only was I top of my class, but I taught myself the ReactJS framework on top of our assignments and lectures, all within the span of a few weeks. I then taught myself React Native, which is outside the curriculum.

My success and my passion are largely due to ideas. I want to add value wherever I may be. I want to see the world change as a result of something I built.
